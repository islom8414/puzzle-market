# Runway Master Prompt

Create a premium short vertical AI animated commercial for Puzzle Market.

Title: The Missing Piece  
Brand: Puzzle Market  
Website: https://www.puzzle-market.com  
Main character: Puzz

Format:
- Vertical video 9:16
- Duration 20-25 seconds
- 1080p or higher
- Clean premium 3D animated look
- No watermark
- Suitable for YouTube Shorts, TikTok, and Instagram Reels

Main character consistency:
Puzz is a cute blue puzzle-piece character with soft rounded edges, big expressive eyes, a kind smile, small white gloves, tiny blue sneakers, and a glowing golden star on his chest. He should look identical in every scene. He is emotional, lovable, and expressive. Premium cinematic 3D family animation, polished materials, soft global illumination, warm highlights, realistic depth of field, smooth camera movement.

Overall mood:
Emotional, warm, hopeful, beautiful, premium, memorable. The ad should feel like a short story, not a normal direct advertisement.

Color palette:
Main blue, clean white, warm golden light, soft wooden backgrounds.

Music:
Warm cinematic soft piano with gentle orchestral lift. Emotional but positive. Build from quiet sadness to hope and joy.

Scene plan:

Scene 1, 0:00-0:04:
On a warm wooden table, a beautiful almost-complete 1000-piece jigsaw puzzle is shown. There is one empty missing space in the center. The room feels cozy, warm, and slightly sad. The camera slowly pushes toward the empty missing space. Soft evening light, shallow depth of field.
On-screen text: Missing one piece?

Scene 2, 0:04-0:08:
Puzz stands alone beside the puzzle. He looks at the empty space and realizes he belongs there. His big eyes become sad, but he remains sweet and gentle. Warm evening lighting, soft shadows, emotional close-up.
On-screen text: Don’t throw it away.

Scene 3, 0:08-0:12:
A smartphone lights up on the wooden table. On the screen, the Puzzle Market website is visible in a clean simplified way. Puzz looks at the phone. His eyes brighten with hope. He smiles softly. Warm glow from the screen reflects on his blue face and golden star.
On-screen text: Find what’s missing.

Scene 4, 0:12-0:17:
A small delivery box arrives at a cozy home. Puzz moves joyfully back toward the puzzle with excitement. Smooth dynamic camera movement. The music becomes inspiring. The animation feels polished, clean, and premium.
On-screen text: Buy. Sell. Complete.

Scene 5, 0:17-0:22:
Puzz carefully jumps into the empty missing spot in the puzzle. A soft satisfying click sound. The puzzle becomes complete. Warm golden light fills the room. Puzz is happy and proud. Emotional payoff, gentle glow, beautiful final composition.
On-screen text: Every Puzzle Deserves To Be Complete.

Scene 6, 0:22-0:25:
Final clean brand card. Dark premium background with warm gold and blue light. Show:
Puzzle Market
www.puzzle-market.com

Important quality rules:
- Keep Puzz visually consistent in all shots.
- Do not make the video look cheap or chaotic.
- Use smooth camera movement and clean composition.
- Avoid distorted text on the website screen. If text becomes unreadable, use a simplified phone UI with Puzzle Market logo and puzzle cards.
- No extra characters.
- No creepy faces, no glitchy hands, no random logos.
- The ad should be understandable without sound, but music should make it emotional.

Generate two versions:
