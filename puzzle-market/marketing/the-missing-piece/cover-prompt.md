# Cover Prompt

Create a vertical 9:16 premium cover image for a short animated ad.

Main subject: Puzz, a cute blue puzzle-piece character with big expressive eyes, a kind smile, small white gloves, tiny blue sneakers, and a glowing golden star on his chest.

Scene: Puzz stands beside a beautiful almost-complete jigsaw puzzle on a warm wooden table. One empty missing space is visible and emotionally important. Warm golden cinematic light, soft depth of field, premium polished 3D animation look, clean composition, high-end advertising quality.

Text on cover:
Missing One Piece?

Composition:
- Puzz on lower right or center-right.
- Missing puzzle space visible on lower left or center-left.
- Text large and readable in the top third.
- Use blue, white, and warm gold.
- No clutter.
- No watermark.
- 1080x1920 or higher.

Negative prompt:
cheap AI look, distorted text, extra limbs, creepy eyes, blurry character, broken puzzle shape, random logos, messy background, unreadable letters, watermark.
